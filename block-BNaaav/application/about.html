About Us

We were established in 2000, in Bangalore, Karnataka. We had started as a small local nursery, and with the love and support from this community, we were able to expand our reach. We believe that having a couple plants around your home or workspace can help immensely in boosting your well-being.

We are a small team of six handling operations for Bloom Hut. Our dream started with founders Jyoti and Mina, who noticed there was a space in the communitiy for a nursery which sells rare and exotic plants at a lower cost.
